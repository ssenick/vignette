Hungarian toll road network is charged via electronic vignette

With a few exceptions, the Hungarian motorways, highways and expressways can only be used with pre-purchased electronic vignette.

Who Must Purchase an electronic vignette before entering Hungary?

The obligation to buy an electronic vignette in Hungary applies to almost all motor vehicles having at least two wheels and a maximum permissible weight of not more than 3,5 tonnes. The buses, motorcycles and towed trailers are also subjects to this obligation.

The Hungarian e-vignette system applies to these vehicle categories:

D1
Passenger cars with max. 7 seats / max. authorised gross weight of 3,5 tons and their trailers belonging to vehicle category D1.
D2
Vans, RVs, minibuses and passenger cars with 8-9 seats / max. authorised gross weight of 3,5 tons.
B2
Buses, i.e. all motor vehicles designed to carry passengers, with more than 9 seats, including the driver.
U
Trailers towed with vehicles of categories D2 and B2.
D1M
Motorcycles.
To define the appropriate vehicle category, please take a look in your vehicle registration certificate.
